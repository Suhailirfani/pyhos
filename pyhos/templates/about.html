About Us

Welcome to Dawa Care Hospital Located in the heart of Elanad, Dawa Care Hospital is committed to providing high-quality, compassionate healthcare to our community. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by a team of experienced and dedicated healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

At Dawa Care Hospital, our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare services with a focus on patient-centered care, innovation, and community well-being. We strive to improve the health and quality of life of those we serve by offering comprehensive medical services and fostering an environment of trust, respect, and collaboration.
